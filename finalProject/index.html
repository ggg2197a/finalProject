Relaxing Physics

Final Project by Greg Gonzalez

Welcome to Relaxing Physics! The goal of this site is to provide small applications with 2D physics that allow for free exploration and enjoyment. Each 'room' has a description of their contents on their page, and it's encouraged that one takes their time and simply does as they please in the room. If a room's contents are somehow thrown outside its walls, please refresh the page to return it to its original state!

For every room, there will be links to the other rooms, and back to this page. The author passes along sincere hopes that you enjoy your time here!
